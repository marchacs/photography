About Me!

Welcome to Marchand Media! Here you can see my work. I specialize in Sports Photography,Graduation Photos, and Family Portraits. On each page you can see my best work from the past year. I have been working close with the James Madison University Football Team as a Creative Media Intern where my boss, Mike Lago, has been helping me develop much stronger photography skills. Some photographers that inspire me are Angelika Johns and Matt Lange from University of Texas Football. I learn a lot by watching others and developing my own techniques.
